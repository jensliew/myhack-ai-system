## SLIDE 5: How It Works - The Workflow (2:15 - 2:45)
**Duration: 30 seconds**

### Visual
- Circular workflow diagram with 4 steps

### Content

**The Nexora Workflow:**

1. **Startup Applies** 
   - Uploads documents & profile
   - Gemini AI extracts & verifies info
   - Admin reviews AI recommendation

2. **AI Matches Mentors**
   - Gemini analyzes compatibility
   - Shows top recommendations with reasoning
   - Mentors can express interest

3. **Startup Selects**
   - Reviews AI suggestions + interested mentors
   - Makes final selection
   - Relationship stored in memory graph

4. **System Learns**
   - Tracks engagement & outcomes
   - Improves future recommendations
   - Knowledge reused across programmes

### Speaker Notes
"Here's how it works. A startup applies and uploads documents. Gemini AI instantly extracts and verifies the information. Then Gemini analyzes compatibility and recommends mentors with reasoning. The startup reviews recommendations and interested mentors, then selects. Every interaction teaches the system to make better matches next time."

## PRESENTATION TIPS

### Timing Breakdown
- Slide 1 (Title): 15 sec
- Slide 2 (Problem): 30 sec
- Slide 3 (Solution): 45 sec
- Slide 4 (Features): 45 sec
- Slide 5 (Workflow): 30 sec
- Slide 6 (Business): 15 sec
- Slide 7 (Impact): 15 sec
- Slide 8 (CTA): 15 sec
- **Total: 3 minutes 10 seconds** (leaves 10 sec buffer)

### Design Recommendations
- Use consistent color scheme (blue/purple for tech, green for success)
- Include Gemini AI logo/badge on relevant slides
- Use icons from Heroicons or Lucide (no emojis in final design)
- Keep text minimal—let visuals tell the story
- Use data visualization for metrics
- Ensure high contrast for readability

### Delivery Tips
1. **Start strong** - Hook with the problem (30 seconds)
2. **Show the solution** - Make it visual and clear
3. **Emphasize AI** - Highlight Gemini's role in each feature
4. **Tell a story** - Problem → Solution → Impact → Action
5. **Practice timing** - Rehearse to hit exactly 3 minutes
6. **Engage audience** - Ask rhetorical questions, pause for effect
7. **End with CTA** - Clear next steps

### Key Messages to Reinforce
- ✅ "Automates ecosystem relationships"
- ✅ "Powered by Google Gemini AI"
- ✅ "Treats relationships as reusable intelligence"
- ✅ "Scales across programmes and countries"
- ✅ "Reduces manual coordination by 80%"
